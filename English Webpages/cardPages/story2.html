Birth Registrations

Getting to know you.

Unfortunately, millions of children around the globe do not officially exist because they are not registered at birth.

Having a name, a nationality and an identity is every child’s right and helps them access essential services, like enrolling in school.

Fortunately, the rates of birth registrations have improved significantly in recent years.

70%
In 2022, over 70% of countries reported improved rates of birth registration.

1 in 4
Globally. One in four children under the age 5 is not registered at birth.

UNICEF works with governments around the world to help them register births.
