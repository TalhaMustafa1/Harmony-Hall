More than half of your body is water. Your blood, your muscles, and even your bones contain water.

Because your body can’t store water, you need to replenish it every day. Having access to safe drinking water is key to a child’s health.

But that’s not all. Safe water and good hygiene practices lower the risk of spreading diseases.

Unsafe water is a leading (and easily preventable!) cause of illness for children under 5.

Safe water leads to better health, nutrition and school attendance-all the things that give a child a fair chance in life.
