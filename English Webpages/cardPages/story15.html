The Power of Play

Acting like a kid helps you grow!

Playing sports, dancing, singing, and activities that help children learn and develop

Through play, children gain imagination and creativity, learn how to connect with others, develop problem-solving skills, and make sense of the world.

In times of trauma, play and sports can help them process their experiences and relieve stress.

In the village of Serednie in Western Ukraine, young people uprooted by war receive mental health support from UNICEF and partners in the form of soccer
Afterward,. The children are invited to make friends and discuss their feelings

There are currently more than 100 million displaced children in the world.
