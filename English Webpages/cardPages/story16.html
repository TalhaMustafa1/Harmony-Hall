Adolescent Kits for Expression and Innovation

Making connections and taking action.

UNICEF’s Adolescent Kit for Expression and Innovation helps teens to connect with each other and build skills for the future.

The Adolescent Kit contains activities and supplies that enable young people to express themselves, experiment, solve real problems, and explore new ideas.

The Adolescent Kit is available in many languages, including French, Arabic, and Spanish.
UNICEF’s team in Algeria even translated the kit into Braille, enabling blind students to fully participate in its activities.

Since its launch in 2016, the Adolescent Kit has reached 1.6 million young people in 45 countries worldwide
