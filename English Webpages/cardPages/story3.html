Nutrition

The ingredients of a strong start

Young children need nutrients to make sure their bodies and brains grow up healthy.

UNICEF helps almost 80 million parents learn about healthy habits, including exclusive breastfeeding for children under six months old, and ways to introduce different food groups to toddlers.

In 2022, UNICEF reached over 350 million children under the age of 5 with programs that prevent malnutrition.

Exclusively breastfeeding children for the first six months of their lives protects them from malnutrition.

UNICEF helps spread the message to new parents.
