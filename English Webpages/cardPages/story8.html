The GIGA Project

Connecting every school in the world to the Internet

UNICEF wants every student to benefit from having the internet in their schools.

A joint initiative between UNICEF and the International Telecommunications Union, Giga’s mission is to connect every school in the world.

How does Giga connect schools to the internet?

Step two: Connect schools to the Internet.

Giga works with governments, internet service providers, and other businesses to help schools get Internet access through negotiating fair prices and investing in networks
